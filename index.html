Building the coolest time-lapse camera ever made.. You'll want one.

Imagine an affordable, solar-powered and weather-proof cube that will take a photo every minute, hour or day.

Use Cases

Things happen too fast for us to realize them. Time-lapse enables exciting insights about change over longer periods of time.

Capturing time in a new format.
Not just photos or videos of a party, wedding or day at the office. A time-lapse is a more fun way to document an event from a certain angle over a longer period of time.

Nature & Development

  • See your garden transforming from winter to summer in a new way.
  • Construction sites are quite active, capture what was going on to see it later fast forward.

Uniqueness

Sure there is many ways to make a time-lapse video. Our approach:

Affordable Gadget

We don't know the final price yet, but our goal is to stay under 100$. Sure you can make awesome stuff with your DSLR, but will you let it sit in your garden for 3 months to capture spring move in?

Smart Design.

Besides being solar-powerd to enable long term projects, we chose a Rhombicuboctahedron for shape, since you then can tilt the camera almost any way you want it. Also each side will have a socket for your favourite tripod.

Easier to use than an actual camera.

Just 3 settings. Taking photos every minute, hour or day. When you are done, connect it to your computer and upload the project folder to our web app. We will let you kick out frames that you don't want in the final version, apply filters and share the outcome with your friends.
